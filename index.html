Basic Findings

Gender Balance

According to the survey, male students outnumber females at almost 2:1.

Subject Ratio

Maths is the most popular subject. English and Maths are tied second.

Grade Ratio

C was the most common grade achieved. A and F were the least.

Average Result by Subject & Gender

Presented below are two graphs which show the group average results achieved by both subject and by gender. The highest subject average comes from students studying English. Maths has the lowest average of the three subjects. Despite being outnumbered by males, female students are achieving higher overall averages in all three subjects.

You can also find the average of each individual subject by individual gender by clicking which gender you want to inspect and vice versa.

Correlation Between Age and Result

A majority of students in all three subjects are under thirty. Males under thirty are the majority group failing to achieve 40% or more in the midterm. Females are the most consistent gender to achieve over 40%. However, males are also the most consistent to achieve 70% or more over females. Mature students over forty are very successful with a majority scoring at least 60% or more.
